## Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails
- Verify website URLs before entering personal information
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone
- Regularly update your apps and software for security
- Be cautious while downloading apps from unknown sources

### 💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID before sending money
- Do not accept unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes

### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
