Cybercrime Awareness Report

While researching cybercrime, I was surprised by how easily people can become victims of online scams, especially through simple actions like clicking on unknown links or sharing OTPs. I realized that many cyberattacks are not very complex but rely on human mistakes and lack of awareness. This made me understand the importance of being cautious while using digital platforms.

One habit I will personally change is being more careful while clicking on links and verifying sources before sharing any personal information. I will also make sure to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication for better security. This task helped me become more aware of online risks and the importance of responsible digital behavior.
