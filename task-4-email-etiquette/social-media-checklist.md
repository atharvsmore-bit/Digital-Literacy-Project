## Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

### ✅ Do's
- Think before you post
- Maintain a positive and respectful tone
- Protect your personal information
- Use strong passwords and privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing

### ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or rumors
- Avoid cyberbullying or negative comments
- Do not misuse someone else's content
