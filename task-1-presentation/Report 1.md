Task 1: Infographic/Poster Creation Report

For this task, I used Canva to create an infographic on digital literacy. Canva is an easy-to-use design tool that provides templates, icons, and layouts, which helped me design my poster efficiently. My design focuses on important aspects of digital literacy such as email etiquette, useful online tools, and safe internet practices. I organized the information in a clear and visually appealing way so that it is easy to understand.

The infographic highlights key points using headings, icons, and simple text to make it informative and engaging. I used different sections to present each topic neatly, which improves readability and presentation quality.

One interesting thing I found while making this design was how different colors, fonts, and layouts can affect the overall look and clarity of the poster. Initially, arranging the content properly was a bit difficult, but Canva’s templates made it easier to structure the design. This task helped me improve my creativity and understanding of digital tools used for communication and presentation.
